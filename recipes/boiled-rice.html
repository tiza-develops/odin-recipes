How to make boiled rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 spoon of oil
  • 1 spoon of salt

Steps

  1. Boil one of the cups of water
  2. Wash the cup of rice and let it drain for an hour
  3. On a saucepan, put the spoon of oil and let the cup of rice turn gold colored with the oil
  4. One it's gold, let the rice cook with 2 cups of water in the saucepan, make sure that the eyelid is closed
  5. Enjoy!
